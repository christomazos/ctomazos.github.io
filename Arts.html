Drama Department

 

We are inspired to create. We are encouraged to perform. We sit back and appreciate. Our love for drama is born. The fine arts of drama allow students to find out who they are as well as other activities. Students learn oral-communication, building self-esteem, and working on public speaking while having fun by studying drama. Lauren hill academy offers drama to all students as an extra-curricular program.

Musical Theater

Senior extra-curricular Broadway musical theatre Lauren hill academy produced a large scale Broadway musical as an extra-curricular activity, together with a group of dedicated students who devote their after school or lunch hours to practicing. This extra-curricular has been going on for the past three years. This extra-curricular has helped a lot of senior students. It has improved their organizational skills, their self-esteem, and they learn time management skills. This extra-curricular also helps the students connect with the teachers more as well as their school. They learn how to see a demanding project through till the end which is very important for teenagers getting ready for life after high-school. The type of school project being put into action also brings together teachers, parents, school administrators, and students all working together to have a great time. This time of school project motivates students to stay in school as well as improve their overall academic success rates.
