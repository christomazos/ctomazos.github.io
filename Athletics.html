Athletics

 

At LaurenHill Academy, we have many sports that students can do. We highly encourage our students to join a sport because it's a very fun experience. The sports that we offer are soccer, rugby, track and field, basketball, volleyball, and badminton. For those who don't want to join a team, our gymnasium is open every lunch, so students can come and play sports and stay active.
