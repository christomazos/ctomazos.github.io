Music

 

At Lauren hill academy music is offered as a fine arts programs to all students in either the French or English program from grade 7 to 11. At Lauren hill academy the music course specializes in teaching music through the jazz/rock idiom. The students will have the opportunity to learn the saxophone, trumpet, trombone, drums, electric bass, and percussion. Students who have previously learned the piano/keyboard or guitar will be able to improve their skills on these instruments. Lauren hill academy offers an after school band as an extra-curricular activity for all students (1 band for each grade level). The students also get the chance to attend concerts, festivals, and other events.
