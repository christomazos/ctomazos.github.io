About

All teachers and staff at LaurenHill Academy are dedicated to providing the best learning experience to our students. We provide a safe learning environment, so that our students are motivated to succeed. With Donna Manos as the head principal, we are proud to be teaching the leaders of the future.

LaurenHill Academy is a Bring Your Own Device (BYOD) school. We let our students use their phones or laptops as a tool to help them succeed. We also believe that technology is the future and students need to learn how to use this technology appropriately and efficiently.
