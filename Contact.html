Contact

We are located at 2505 boulevard Côte Vertu Ville-St-Laurent, QC H4R 1P3. If you have any questions, feel free to call us at (514) 331-8781 or fax us at (514) 331-7145. LaurenHill Academy is also easily accessible by the 121 bus that passes every 10 minutes. If you wish to contact a specific teacher, you can email them. Their emails are the first letter of their first name and then their last name, which is then followed by @emsb.qc.ca. (For example: Mr. Tzortis’s full name is Leon Tzortis. His email is ltzortis@emsb.qc.ca)
